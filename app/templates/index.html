Dreamtime Bush Trail

Welcome to the Dreamtime Bush Trail at Goondoi wetlands.
This half-kilometre walk, co-designed by Traditional Owners and Radiant Life College students, features interpretive signs, names, images, and artworks that share the stories and culture of this special place.

This project is funded by the Great Barrier Reef Foundation.
