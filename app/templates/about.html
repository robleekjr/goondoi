Dreamtime Bush Trail

Dreamtime Bush Trail, a project funded by the Great Barrier Reef Foundation, is located at the Goondoi wetlands and is in partnership with the Radiant Life College secondary students.

The Dreamtime Trail is a half-kilometre bush trail walk through the wonderful wetlands, where interpretive signs, names, images, and artworks all co-designed by Traditional Owners can be found.
